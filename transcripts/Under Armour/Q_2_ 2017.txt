And finally, our Connected Fitness business was down 2% to $23 million.

Second quarter operating loss was $5 million and interest and other income expense was $11 million. Our tax rate was 21% in the quarter compared to 41% last year due to discrete items taken in certain foreign markets.
Taking all of this to the bottom line, we had a net loss of $12 million in the second quarter, or a loss of $0.03 in diluted earnings per share.
