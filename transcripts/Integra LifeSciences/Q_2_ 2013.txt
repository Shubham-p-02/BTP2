For revenue, though, the full-year 2013 results are probably a good place to start and then grow off of that within our long-term guidance range of 5% to 7%. Rather than trying to jump off the fourth quarter, we suggest you jump off the total 2013 revenue estimates.

No doubt many of you will ask the same question on the October call and we may have something more to say, although we're going to give our guidance for the year, obviously, in February, as we always do.
PETER ARDUINI: I would just comment, Larry, the fact is, and we were reiterating this about the wide range for this year, is the fact that with three sites we believe we're going to have inspections completed on, that gives a wide range of views. And obviously, that drives a lot of subsequent decisions about investment or cost. As they go well, there are key items that we clearly want to accelerate, as Jack commented on, that really are about changing the motif of the company for the long run and really changing the margin and growth trajectories for the long run.

Instruments, we've had some weakness in our off-site area, we've had some strength in acute care, but as I've mentioned in the comments, a larger chunk of it was specifically tied to, we actually got rid of some lower margin products within our portfolio, which will have a positive effect, obviously, as we go forward.
