So the good thing about it is we have got numerous groups saying the same thing we have been saying for 17 months trying to get clarity. And understanding why this happened to us as a standalone and nothing else has transpired along these same lines for 17 months until this request for additional guidance on this guidance document came out.
So we are looking forward to some clarity. Industry I think can deal with most anything as long as there is some logic and some clarity to it. It is when it is confusing and answers aren't coming forward and those kinds of things and that is kind of the situation we have been in.
