OVERVIEW
SLB reported 1Q09 EPS from continuing operations, excluding charges and credits, of $0.78, down $0.28 vs. 1Q08 and down $0.25 sequentially.
FINANCIAL DATA
A. Key Data From Call 1. 1Q09 EPS from continuing operations (excluding charges and credits) = $0.78. 2. YoverY EPS from continuing operations decline = $0.28 vs. 1Q08. 3. Sequential EPS from continuing operations decline = $0.25. 4. 1Q09 CapEx = $748m. 5. 1Q09-end cash and investments on hand = approx. $4.4b. 6. 1Q09-end net debt = $1.5b.
